I have chosen the CSS options for more practice on the various different CSS possibilities.

  • Linear Gradients- I used a gray gradient background on my body and a green gradient on my container div.
  • Multi-column Layouts- The layout starts as one vertical layout on mobile, then at the 768px breakpoint turns into a 2 column layout, then at the 1100px breakpoint turns into a 3 column layout.
  • Box Shadow- I added a box shadow on my container div article block to give it a cool sort of 3d look, and a strong contrast to the background.
  • Alpha Transparency- For my container div, I changed the background to a dark green with an Alpha Transparency of 30%
  • Use a media-query to swap in a high-resolution image(s) on a high-res device- For the smaller screen viewport up to the 1100px breakout, I have 2 images. Then at the 1100px breakout the images swap out into 2 different ones.
  • CSS structural pseudo-classes- For the pseudo class, I put an Nth child on my container div, with the purpose of changing the color of just one of my captions (this is some cool stuff! into red).
